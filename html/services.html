Our

Services

 

Renovations

No reno too big or too small. We can redesign your bathroom, change the whole layout of your main floor or finish your basement.

Carpentry

We have a professional who can install and build bookcases, fireplace mantels and more. We can do but, are not limited to, crown molding, wainscoting, ceiling designs and trim.

 

Plumbing

Our licensed plumber can fix leaky faucet or change an old toilet all the way to installing a new sink or changing the location of your plumbing.

Electrical

Our licensed electritian can do small projects such as installing ceiling fans or light dimmers all the way to larger projects  like installing pot lights, rewiring your home to meet code, and adding new wires to place a light or receptacle where you wish.

Duct Work/Mechanical

We can replace your furnace, add in an air conditioner or just simply fix or run new duct lines. We can also install a gas line to your fireplace, stove or BBQ.

Windows and Doors

We can replace, install new or remove exisiting windows and doors.

 

New Builds

Do you have a piece of property you want to build your dream home on? We can take care of all the steps and processes to make that dream a home. Right from the surveys, digging and foundation to the finishing touches inside.

Painting

Our painters are dedicated to creating your home with the colour schemes of your choice. They have great design and colour concepts that will fit your character. We ensure the walls are smooth, prepped and cleaned before painting to allow for a good canvas. 

 

Landscaping

Do you want to redesign or change your outdoor space? We provide concrete and interlocking work. This includes but does not limit to, pattern concrete, design concrete, foundation for porches, retaining walls and garden beds.

Flooring

We can replace your floors in any room, office or building. We have professional installers for hardwood, tiles and carpet.

Stairs and Railings

We can replace your existing stairs and railings or simply change the stain or add or remove a runner.
